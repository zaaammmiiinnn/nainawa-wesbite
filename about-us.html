About Nainawa Ziyarat Tours

Guiding pilgrims on sacred journeys since 2008

Our Story

Nainawa Ziyarat Tours was founded in 2011 by a group of religious scholars and travel experts who recognized the need for organized, spiritually enriching pilgrimage experiences to the holy sites of Iraq and Iran.

What began as a small operation arranging trips for local communities has grown into an internationally recognized pilgrimage travel agency, serving thousands of pilgrims from all over India.

Our name "Nainawa" pays homage to the historical region surrounding Karbala, connecting our mission to the sacred geography we serve.

Our first group of pilgrims in Damascus, Syria 2011

Our Journey

Foundation

2008

Established in Lucknow by Shabahat abbas Rizvi and a group of local scholars to serve pilgrims visiting Imam Ali's (a.s.) shrine

First International Group

2011

Organized our first international pilgrimage group from Lucknow, India

Arbaeen Expansion

2012

Launched specialized Arbaeen walk packages, becoming one of the first agencies to organize the Najaf-Karbala walk for international pilgrims

Iran Operations

2014

Expanded services to include nainawa ziyarat tours in Iran, establishing partnerships with shrines in Mashhad and Qom

Recognition

2019

Received official endorsement from the Office of Ayatollah Sistani for our authentic religious programming

Present Day

2025

Serving over 1,000+ pilgrims annually with a team of 10+ dedicated staff members across Iraq and Iran

Our Values

Service to the Ahlulbayt (a.s.)

We consider facilitating ziyarat as a form of service to the Ahlulbayt (a.s.), not just a business. Our team is trained to treat every pilgrim with the respect due to visitors of the holy shrines.

Authentic Scholarship

All our religious guides are graduates of recognized hawzas and our programs are reviewed by senior scholars to ensure authentic representation of Islamic teachings.

Safety First

We maintain rigorous safety standards, with 24/7 security coordination, emergency medical support, and thorough risk assessments for all our programs.

Community Focus

A portion of our profits supports local communities near the holy shrines, including orphanages, schools, and public facilities used by pilgrims.
